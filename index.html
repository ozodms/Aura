Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer warranties on your watches?
Yes, all our watches come with a standard [1-year/2-year] manufacturer&rsquos warranty, covering any defects in materials or craftsmanship. For more details, please refer to our warranty policy.
Can I return or exchange a watch if Im not satisfied?
Absolutely! We offer a [14-day/30-day] return and exchange policy. The watch must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by the purchase receipt. Custom or engraved watches are not eligible for return.
Do you provide watch repair or battery replacement services?
Yes, we offer professional watch repair and battery replacement services. Bring your watch to our store or contact us to schedule a service.
Are your watches authentic?
Yes, all our watches are 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized dealers or trusted suppliers. We do not sell replicas or counterfeit products.
Do you size watch bracelets or adjust straps?
Yes, we provide complimentary bracelet resizing for watches purchased from our store. For watches purchased elsewhere, a small fee may apply.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. Shipping fees and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please check our shipping policy for details.
Do you sell limited edition or pre-owned watches?
Yes, we carry a selection of limited edition and pre-owned watches. Each pre-owned watch is thoroughly inspected for quality and authenticity before being offered for sale.
